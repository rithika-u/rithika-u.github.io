How It Works

🎮 Game Controls

Movement: Arrow Keys or WASD

Start: Press Space or click Start

Pause: Press P

Power-up: Collect roses 🌹 to shoot hearts 💕

📚 Paper Search

Type keywords in the search box to filter papers by:

• Paper titles

• Author names

• Abstract content

Hit Enter or click Search to find papers!

💡 Tips & Tricks

Game: Use the grace period (5 sec) to collect dots before ghosts start moving!

Papers: Search for topics like "neural networks", "machine learning", or "reinforcement learning"
